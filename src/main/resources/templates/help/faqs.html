What should I do if the details on my finance agreement are wrong? +

Contact the retailer where you bought your goods from and we'll work with them to make sure your paperwork and any mistakes are put right as quickly as possible.

What if I have a problem with my goods? +

Please contact the retailer first to discuss the issue. If the matter is unresolved, contact our customer services team.

What can I do if I'm not happy with something in my finance agreement or the service I receive from Azadi Financial Services? +

We take all complaints seriously. Please contact our customer services team and we will do our best to resolve the issue.

Why is my current balance larger than I was expecting? +

Your balance includes any interest that has accrued since your last payment. Please contact us if you need a detailed breakdown.

How will my payments show up on my bank statement? +

Your payments will appear as "Azadi Financial Services" or "AFS" on your bank statement.

Can I change my payment date? +

Yes, you can change your payment date once during the lifetime of your agreement. Please note this may incur additional interest.

Why are you charging me for changing my payment date? +

Changing your payment date extends the period between payments which means additional interest may accrue during that time.
