Where can I find my agreement number?

Please note that your agreement number can be found:

  • In the finance agreement you received from the retailer, a copy of which you should have received from us in the post.
  • In the reference field of your Direct Debit agreement with us, this should be accessible to you via your bank online.
  • In the introductory SMS you received from us at the start of your agreement.
